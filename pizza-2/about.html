My Story

My Beginning

I've got an interesting story you see. I would make pizza for myself every Friday. I called it pizza Friday. It was amazing! My first couple pizzas were not very good. But with some time and practice I actually got quite good. Well, one day I started showing some of my friends what great pizza I could make.

My Friends

My Friends loved my pizza so much that they said I should open up my own restaurant! At first I was pretty skeptical. A restaurant? Me? I don't know, that seems like a lot of work!

Over time, as I kept making my pizzas every friday I couldn't shake the possibility from my mind. "Maybe it could work!" I would think.

My Growth

In the end I decided I would experiment with the idea of selling my pizza. I would go to local hot spots with lots of foot traffic and offer my pizza for a dollar a slice. Suprisingly, my pizza was a hit! The people could not have enough of it!

My Restaurant

Well, one thing led to another and BOOM! I went all in! I gathered all of my life savings together to create my very own pizza restaurant. After leasing out restaurant space, word got around on what I was doing. Some of my fans who had tried my pizza found out I was opening up my own place and started spreading rumors like wild fire.

The day I opened up shop so many people came to try out my pizza! Wow, it was such an exciting day!

My Pizza

Since the people keep coming, I remain open! Giving away what started out as a personal weekly event.
